About Us

Welcome to NYP Chess Club

Founded in 2025 by Andy Lim, the NYP Chess Club brings together chess enthusiasts from across the campus. Whether you’re new to chess or already a competitive player, you’ll find a supportive and vibrant community here.

NYP Chess Club was set up by Andy Lim in 2025. At that time, NYP did not have a chess club. Being a chess lover, Andy decided to start the club so that others could benefit from the beauty of chess.

The club is a place for any NYP student who wants to learn chess or improve their skills. Whether you’re just starting out or already a strong player, we welcome you.

Chess is not just any board game—it helps improve your analytical skills and lets you relax at the same time. At our weekly meetups, we interact, learn, and play with one another.

Don’t hesitate to join us now!

Our Journey

  • 2025 – NYP Chess Club founded by Andy Lim
  • March 2025 – First weekly meetup held
  • June 2025 – Won our first InterPoly Title
  • July 2025 – Reached 100+ members
  • August 2025 – Hosted NYP NDP Chess Competition
